Our School Project

In response to the pressing need for increased awareness and understanding of climate change, our team has developed the Awareness Climate Change Simulator. This innovative project aims to fill a crucial gap in online resources by providing a unique platform that allows users to simulate and explore the impact of various climate-related policies. The simulator enables users to delve into the complexities of climate change's consequences on a global scale.

Our motivation for creating this simulator stemmed from the absence of a comprehensive online tool dedicated to fostering awareness of climate change through interactive earth globe. Recognizing the urgency of addressing climate issues, our project seeks to engage users in an immersive experience that not only educates but also empowers them to make informed decisions towards a sustainable future.
